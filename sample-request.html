Sample Requests

SEE IT BEFORE YOU SCALE.

Planning a large order? Request sample garments, decoration swatches, or product options before committing to the full run.

Blank Samples

Check fit, fabric weight, color, and feel before choosing the final garment.

Decoration Samples

Review embroidery, screen print, DTG, foil, or sublimation quality for your project.

Bulk Buyers

Best for events, teams, companies, schools, and brands ordering 100+ units.

Sample Cost

Blank samples are quoted by item. Decorated samples may include setup, decoration, and shipping, depending on the product and method.

Turnaround

Most blank sample requests can be planned quickly. Decorated samples take longer because artwork, proofing, and production setup are involved.

Bulk Order Credit

For qualifying larger orders, approved sample costs may be credited toward the final production invoice when the full order is placed.

What We Can Sample

Popular tees, hoodies, hats, totes, mugs, embroidery swatches, print samples, label options, and comparable product alternatives.

What To Include

Tell us the expected quantity, deadline, product style, colors, sizing needs, decoration method, and whether you need blank or decorated samples.

Best Timing

Request samples before finalizing sizes, colors, and budget. Rush samples are possible only when blanks and artwork are ready.
