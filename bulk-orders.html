Common Questions

Bulk Order FAQ

What's the minimum for a bulk order?
Our bulk pricing starts at 48 units for the Smart Pro tier. For enterprise pricing (from $6/unit), minimums start at 500 units. Smaller orders from 12 units are available at standard pricing — see our pricing page.
How long does a bulk order take?
Standard turnaround is 3–7 business days for bulk orders depending on size and method. Rush production (48 hours from approval) is available on most bulk orders for an additional fee.
Can you ship to multiple addresses?
Yes — our drop-ship service can ship to any number of individual addresses. Just provide us with the list and we'll handle all the packaging and fulfillment.
What if I need different sizes or colors?
Size and color breakdowns within a single order are standard. Just provide your size run (e.g. 20 S / 30 M / 30 L / 20 XL) when you submit your quote request.
Do you offer ongoing fulfillment for e-commerce?
Yes. We can store your inventory and fulfill individual orders as they come in — great for merch stores, subscription boxes, and branded gifting programs. Contact us to discuss your setup.
What's included in the quote?
Your quote will include per-unit pricing, setup fees (if any), shipping cost to your location(s), production timeline, and payment terms. No hidden fees added later.

Ready to Place a Bulk Order?

Fill out our quote form and we'll get back to you same business day with pricing, mockups, and a timeline. No commitment required.
