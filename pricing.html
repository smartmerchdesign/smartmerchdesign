Minimums

Order Size Guidelines

Minimums vary by product and decoration method. These notes help you choose the right path before requesting final pricing.

Screen Print

12+ Units

Best value for apparel runs with the same artwork. Mix sizes freely; garment colors and ink colors can affect setup and pricing.

DTG / Full Color

1+ Unit

Useful for samples, small batches, and complex full-color art. Per-piece pricing is higher, but setup is simpler.

Embroidery

12+ Units

Strong for hats, polos, bags, jackets, and premium apparel. Digitizing is reviewed with the artwork before production.

Promo Products

Varies By Item

Mugs, pens, stickers, bottles, and specialty items each have different vendor minimums. We confirm realistic options in your quote.

Samples

Best For 100+ Unit Plans

Physical samples make the most sense when you are comparing blanks or decoration quality before a larger order.

Rush Orders

Depends On Availability

Rush timing depends on blank inventory, artwork readiness, proof approval, decoration method, quantity, and shipping distance.
