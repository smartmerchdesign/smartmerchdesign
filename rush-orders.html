Rush Orders

NEED MERCH FAST?

Rush production is possible when the blanks, artwork, proof approval, decoration method, and shipping plan line up. Tell us the real deadline and we will confirm what is realistic before you commit.

Rush Checklist

What Makes A Rush Work

Artwork Ready

Clean Files Move Faster

Vector art or high-resolution files reduce proofing delays. Low-resolution logos may need cleanup before production.

Blank Availability

In-Stock Products Win

Common tees, hoodies, hats, and totes are easier to rush than specialty colors, premium cuts, or vendor-limited items.

Fast Approval

Proofs Need A Quick Yes

Production starts after final proof approval. Rush jobs work best when one decision-maker can approve changes quickly.

Simple Decoration

Fewer Variables Help

One print location, fewer colors, standard placement, and proven methods are faster than complex multi-location setups.

Quantity Fit

Scale Affects Timing

A 24-piece rush order and a 500-piece rush order are very different. We will confirm capacity before promising a date.

Shipping Plan

Delivery Counts Too

Rush production does not replace transit time. Local pickup or express shipping may be needed for tight event dates.

Timing

Rush Timeline Reality Check

  • 48-72 hours: Possible for select simple apparel orders after approved proof, depending on inventory and production capacity.
  • 3-5 business days: More realistic for many rush apparel, hat, tote, and repeat orders with ready artwork.
  • Specialty products: Promotional items, custom packaging, labels, and multi-location orders may need more time.
  • Hard event dates: Share the in-hand date, shipping ZIP code, and whether pickup is possible.
