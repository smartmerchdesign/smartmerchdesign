💡 The Process

FROM IDEA TO DOORSTEP IN 5 SMART STEPS

No confusion. No chasing. Just a clear, streamlined process that turns your vision into reality — fast and stress-free.

01
Step One Takes about 5 minutes
💡 Share Your Vision

Start by telling us what you need — products, quantities, timeline, and any brand assets you have. The more info you share, the faster we can build something great. Use our quote form, shoot us an email, or give us a call. Zero commitment required.

Free, no-obligation quote Share your logo, colors, ideas Tell us your timeline & budget We respond same business day
02
Step Two 24–48 hours after inquiry
✏️ We Build Your Mockup

Our designers get to work creating a digital mockup of your product — exactly how it'll look in real life. We'll share it for your review and make as many revisions as you need. You don't pay a dime until you love it.

Digital mockup provided Unlimited free revisions Color, size, placement options Final proof approval required
03
Step Three Once you love the proof
💳 Approve & Pay

When you're happy with the proof, we send a final order summary and invoice. A 50% deposit kicks off production. It's that simple — no mystery fees, no fine print. Just the number we quoted you.

Clear, itemized invoice 50% deposit to start Credit card, PayPal, or bank transfer Balance due on completion
04
Step Four 7–10 days standard · 48hr rush
🏭 We Produce Your Order

Your order goes into production using the right printing method for the job — screen print, DTG, embroidery, or sublimation. Every piece is quality-checked before it goes anywhere near a shipping box.

Right print method selected Premium blanks sourced QC inspection on every item Rush option available
05
Step Five Fast, tracked, insured
📦 Ship Smart

Your finished order is packed carefully and shipped with full tracking. We send you the tracking info the moment it ships. Delivery to one address or a thousand — we handle it all with care.

Tracked shipping Expedited options available Multi-location drop-ship Balance collected on ship
